# Content Strategy

The platform serves both:

### Beginners

Goals:

- Learn AI fundamentals
- Understand approved tools
- Find guided learning paths

### Power Users

Goals:

- Discover advanced techniques
- Share expertise
- Reuse AI skills and prompts

## Common Failure Modes

Most AI hubs fail because:

- They become document repositories
- Ownership is unclear
- Content becomes stale
- Success stories are missing

Mitigation:

- Clear ownership
- Quarterly reviews
- Community contributions
- Usage analytics
