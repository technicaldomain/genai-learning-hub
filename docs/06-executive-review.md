# Executive Review

## Why This Portal Exists

The goal is not to create another documentation site.

The goal is to enable safe, scalable, and sustainable AI adoption across an organization.

---

## Success Criteria

- Employees discover AI tools faster
- AI skills become reusable
- Teams share successful patterns
- AI knowledge becomes self-service

---

## Key Design Decisions

### AI Enablement Platform

Chosen over traditional learning portal.

Reason:

Focus on outcomes instead of content volume.

### AI Skills Marketplace

Added to promote discoverability of reusable automations.

### OIDC Authentication

Demonstrates enterprise readiness.

### API-Driven Architecture

Supports future expansion.

---

## Risks

### Low Adoption

Mitigation:

- Community champions
- Success stories
- Fresh content

### Stale Content

Mitigation:

- Ownership model
- Review cadence
